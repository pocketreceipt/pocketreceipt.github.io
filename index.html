Why ALERT?
In the midst of the 2016 U.S. Presedential election, we witnessed a prolifferation of fake news disseminate all over the internet. In efforts to push political propaganda, skew public opinion and spread false or missrepresented information, thousands of fake websites and articles were created and shared on social media platforms.

This type of fictitious information can be very harmful, especially when targeted towards specific people. It strengthens divides between people and absorbs attention away from the truth. In the wake of this epidemic, while taking advantage of those vulnerable, these fabricated stories have also created a rift in the trust that exists between the media and the public.

With a sincere concern for this issue, we created Alert!, an all purpose news platform created to keep you up to date on the things you care about, while ensuring that what you read comes from credited, verified and reliable news sources. Truthful information is a right and therefore must be protected and that is our goal. We have a number of measures in place that allow us both to block false content and handle user complaints about given articles to better monitor disguised fake content.

Download Alert now and join the fight against fake news. We're a student led company, sponsored by the Latino Muslim Student Aliance, passionate about creating change in any way we can. Start reading great articles and always, Stay ALERT!
